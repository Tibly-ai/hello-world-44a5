The story

Two words, fifty years of momentum

"Hello, World!" first appeared in a 1972 tutorial at Bell Labs and was made famous by Brian Kernighan and Dennis Ritchie's book The C Programming Language in 1978. It was never meant to be profound — just proof that the machine was listening.

But that's exactly why it stuck. Before you build anything grand, you need one small, honest success. A blinking confirmation that says: you're connected, keep going. This page is that confirmation, published to the open web.
